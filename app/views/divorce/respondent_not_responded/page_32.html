Requesting a service pack

A service pack contains all the divorce documents that need to be ‘served’ (sent) to your husband or wife. You need a service pack if you want to serve the divorce papers yourself, or if you want a professional (e.g process server) to serve them for you.

Requesting a service pack does not cost any additional fees.
