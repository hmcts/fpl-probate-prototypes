Applying for ‘deemed service’

If you have evidence that your husband or wife has received the divorce application by email or post, then you can apply for ‘deemed service’.

If your application is successful then the court will accept your husband or wife has been served with the application. The court will then allow your application to continue without a response from them.

You should only apply for deemed service if you have evidence they received all the divorce papers. For example, a ‘certificate of service’, a proof of delivery or a message from them saying they have received all the documents.

The application for deemed service costs £53, unless you are eligible for Help With Fees.
