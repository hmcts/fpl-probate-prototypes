Applying to search government records

You are about to apply to search government records for your [husband’s / wife’s / civil partner’s] contact details.

It can take several months to search government records. You should therefore only apply if you have no way of sending the application to your [husband / wife/ civil partner].

If you have any other contact details for them then you can apply to ‘serve’ (send) the application to them in that way. For example to their email address, mobile phone number or social media profile. You can apply to serve them another way here.

If you still want to apply to search government records, then it will cost an additional £53, unless you are eligible for Help With Fees.
