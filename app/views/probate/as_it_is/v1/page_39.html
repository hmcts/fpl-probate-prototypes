Which executors will be dealing with the estate?

Up to 4 executors can be named on the grant of probate.

  • collect the estate’s assets (property, money and possessions)
  • pay any taxes and debts
  • distribute what’s left to the people entitled to it (the ‘beneficiaries’)

You’ll be asked about executors who won’t be dealing with the estate later in the application.
