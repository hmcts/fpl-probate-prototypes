You need to send us the original will

The will you sent us is not the original. By law, we need to see the original will and keep it as it becomes public record.

An original, legal will must be in writing and have wet signatures of the deceased and both witnesses.

What you need to do

Send us the original will, preferably by ‘tracked with signature’, to:

HMCTS Probate
PO Box 12625
Harlow
CM209QE

Make a copy of the will for your records.

Helpful Information

If the original was made with a solicitor it may be held at their office. If the firm of solicitors that drew up the original are no longer in existence you’ll need to contact the Solicitors Regulation Authority (opens in a new tab) to search their records as they may now hold the original will.
