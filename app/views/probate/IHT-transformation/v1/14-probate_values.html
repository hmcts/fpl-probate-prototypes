What are the values of the estate for probate?

The values you enter must be whole numbers and must not include decimals, full stops or commas. For example 6000


This is the total value of the assets the person who died held in their name, not including assets held jointly with anyone else.

To work out the gross value for probate, take the gross value of the estate for Inheritance Tax then take away the values of any joint assets passing to the other joint owner, any foreign assets, all gifts and any assets held in trust.

To work this out, take the gross value for probate you calculated above and take away the total value of all the debts the person who died owed in their name, including funeral expenses, at the time of their death. Do not include debts owed jointly with other people.
