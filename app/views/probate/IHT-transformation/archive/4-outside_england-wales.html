You must apply for probate by post

If the person who died didn’t live in England or Wales or intend to return here, you can’t apply for probate online.

If the person who died:

  • left a will, fill in Form PA1P (opens in a new tab).
  • did not leave a will, fill in Form PA1A (opens in a new tab).
