Do you have a death certificate or an interim death certificate?

A death certificate is printed double-sided on green paper and is given to you by a registrar from the births, deaths and marriages registry.

An interim death certificate (opens in a new tab) is only issued by the coroner if there is an inquest to find out the cause of death.
