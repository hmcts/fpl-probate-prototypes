Respond to the caseworker’s comments

Read the caseworker’s reasons for stopping the application and provide the requested information.

The caseworker's comments:

You have told us that the will is lost.

You'll need to complete Form PA13 to give us more information about the lost will to support your application. You can download and complete this form from www.gov.uk/government/publications/report-a-will-is-lost-to-support-a-probate-application or by calling 0117 9302430 asking for Form PA13.

Complete and send the form to us with any relevant documents and supporting evidence.


Write your response if the caseworker wants you to explain something or provide additional information. If the caseworker has only asked you to upload documents, you do not have to write anything unless you think it’s useful information.


You can upload documents if the caseworker has asked you to provide any, or if you have any evidence to support your response.

Make sure the photo or scan shows the whole document. Check you can read all the text before uploading it. If court staff cannot read it, the document may be rejected.
You can take a picture with your phone and upload it
  1. Place your document on a flat service in a well-lit room. Use a flash if you need to.
  2. Take a picture of the whole document. You should be able to see its edges.
  3. Email or send the photo or scan to the device you are using now.
  4. Upload it using the link below.

You should upload at least one clear image which shows the whole document. If you think it will help court staff read the details, you can upload more images. If your document has more than one page, you should upload at least one image of each page.
