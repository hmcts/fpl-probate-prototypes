Apply for probate

Complete these steps to get the legal right to deal with the property and belongings of someone who has died.

You can save your application and return to it later if you need to.

At the end of each section you will be required to check your answers. If any are incorrect you will have the opportunity to change them before proceeding to the next section. Once you have made your legal declaration at section 3 any amendments will have to be made in writing to the Registry.
