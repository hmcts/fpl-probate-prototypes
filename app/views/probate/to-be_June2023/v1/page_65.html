Send your documents

1. Prepare to send your documents

You must send the probate registry:

  • either your completed application coversheet or write your reference number 1667-4002-6989-2539 on a sheet of paper
  • the original will (by law, we nmust keep your original will as it becomes a public document)
  • please send any page or document that you have removed from the will
We cannot give you a grant of probate without the required documents.

2. Send your documents to the probate registry

Send your documents using a signed postal service to:

Principal Registry of the Family Division (PRFD)
HMCTS Probate
PO Box 12625
Harlow
CM20 9QE
