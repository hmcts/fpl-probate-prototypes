How many past and present executors are named on the will and any updates (‘codicils’)?

The total number must include anyone who:

  • is a current executor, even if they aren’t applying for probate
  • was meant to be an executor but can’t be one (for example, if they’ve died and have been replaced by another executor)
Do not include anyone who has not yet become an executor.
